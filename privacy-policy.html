Privacy Policy

This privacy policy sets out how our website uses and protects any information that you give when you use this website.

Analytics

We use Matomo, a web analytics software, to track and analyze user behavior on our website. Matomo helps us understand how visitors engage with our site and allows us to improve our services.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website, Matomo collects certain information automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website
  • Date and time of visit
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on each page

This information is used for statistical purposes only and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Opting Out

If you do not want Matomo to track your visits, you can opt out by disabling cookies in your browser settings.
